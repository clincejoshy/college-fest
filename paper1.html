SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

Papers that do not comply with the Submission Guidelines will be rejected without review.

• Authors are strongly advised to understand about plagiarism before submitting their manuscript. The authors will take the sole responsibility for all the     issues related to plagiarism, if any.
• All papers must be submitted electronically through the paper submission system in MS-Word compatible (.doc, .docx) format only.
• Once the paper is uploaded successfully, our Paper Submission System assigns a Unique Paper ID and sends an acknowledgement email to the      author. The authors must quote /refer the paper ID in all future correspondences.
• The total length of the paper, including references and appendices, must not exceed six pages in double column, single-line spacing, 10 font size,    Times New Roman. DO NOT INCLUDE headers and footers in the paper.
• Manuscripts must be clearly presented in English. To avoid unnecessary errors, the authors are strongly advised to use the "spell-check" and    "grammar-check" functions of the word processor.
•  Authors are also requested to use the paper template (click here to download) for the preparation of full length paper.
• Each individual author is limited to a maximum of three submissions as an author or co-author. At least one author of each accepted paper MUST    register and present the paper in the conference.
• Online presentation facilities are also available for authors who are from outside India if they are unable to attend the conference. The submission    deadline is firm and not negotiable.

Publishing process

• Organizing committee performs a pre reviewing (adherence to the submission guidelines and English language usage) and papers will be assigned to   distinguished reviewers for refereeing.
• The Organizing Committee will send the review reports to authors for paper improvement once at least two productive review reports are received.
• Author will be required to revise paper and update the revised version. If necessary, revised paper will be sent to the original reviewers for the second   round of review.
• Acceptance notification will be sent to the corresponding author.
• Author should pay the registration fee, and upload the final version of paper (Camera ready). Final version of paper will be proofread by the Organizing   Committee. Kindly do check and correct your paper carefully when proofs are sent to you.

Selected peer reviewed full papers will be published in the following UGC approved journals:
• International Journal of Computer Sciences and Engineering
  ISSN: 2347-2693 Website: http://www.ijcseonline.org/
• International Journal of Scientific Research in Network Security and Communication
  ISSN: 2321-3256 Website: http://www.ijsrnsc.org/

Selected Special articles from the conference will be invited for extension to be re-reviewed for inclusion in the special issue of SCIE, DBLP & SCOPUS indexed journals
