REGISTRATION DETAILS

Registration

At least one author of an accepted paper is required to register. If an author has got more than one accepted papers, each paper need to be registered. Registration fee will include conference kit with proceedings, lunch and refreshments. Accommodation and transportation charges not included.


Payment Methods

NEFT/RTGS/ Net transfer. (No credit card payment method). Make sure, Author name and Paper ID is clearly mentioned in the Funds Transfer Application. Students (UG/PG Students and only Full time Research/PhD Scholars) have to submit their scanned copy of the ID card along with the Registration form. Original Student ID card and Bonafide certificate from the Head of the Institution/Department need to be produced at the time of conference.
