SUBMISSION GUIDELINES

Papers that do not comply with the Submission Guidelines will be rejected without review. Please note: All papers must be submitted electronically through the paper submission system in Word or PDF format only. Paper size should be 8-12 pages. Papers must be clearly presented in English Each individual author is limited to a maximum of three submissions as an author or co-author. At least one author of each accepted paper is required to attend the conference and present the paper. DO NOT INCLUDE headers and footers in the paper. Online presentation facilities are also available for authors who are from outside India if they are unable to attend the conference. The submission deadline is firm and not negotiable.


Publishing process

Authors submit papers via the online submission system. Paper format should be in Word. Organizing committee performs a pre reviewing (adherence to the submission guidelines and English language usage) and papers will be assigned to distinguished reviewers for refereeing. The Organizing Committee will send the review reports to authors for paper improvement once at least two productive review reports are received. Author will be required to revise paper and update the revised version. If necessary, revised paper will be sent to the original reviewers for the second round of review. Acceptance notification will be sent to the corresponding author. Author should pay the registration fee, and upload the final version of paper (Camera ready). Final version of paper will be proofread by the Organizing Committee. Kindly do check and correct your paper carefully when proofs are sent to you.
